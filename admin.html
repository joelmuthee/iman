WhatsApp Marketing
⏳ Why this sends one message at a time WhatsApp bans numbers that send lots of messages too fast. Going one buyer at a time, each with a personal message, is exactly what keeps your number safe. Take it steady, don't rush it, and never use bulk-blast apps. A calm pace protects your number and your business.

Pick items, compose a message and send to past buyers. Manual mode for now — clicks open WhatsApp Web pre-filled with the message and the buyer's number. Connect WA Business API + GHL later to make this one-click.

Clients

Everyone who's bought from you, with what they bought and how to reach them. Save a buyer's name and phone when you record a sale, and they'll appear here so you can message them when new stock lands.

Expenses

Track what you spend running the shop — Instagram ads, packaging, transport. See your real profit after costs on the Sales dashboard. These figures are private and never shown on your store.

+ Add an expense

Recurring spend is an estimate that grows automatically. For Instagram, where Meta deducts different amounts every few days, logging each actual deduction as a one-off is more exact.

Money owed

Customers who took items but haven't paid in full yet. When you record a sale and they pay less than the price, the rest shows up here so you can keep track and chase it. Record a payment whenever they pay you something.

Loyalty Program

Reward repeat buyers. Customers build up automatically from the name and phone you save when recording a sale — nothing extra to type. Read a customer their progress at the counter, or tap WhatsApp to nudge them. None of this shows on the public site.
