Iman High Street is a Nairobi shop for brand new fashion, for her and for him. We stock dresses, skirt suits, blazers, tops, heels and pure Italian leather handbags for the ladies, plus quality suits, ties and leather shoes for the gentlemen. We negotiate prices, not quality.
